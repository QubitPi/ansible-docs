Ansible collections

Collections are a distribution format for Ansible content that can include playbooks, roles, modules, and plugins.

Ansible plugins and modules are available from collections. If you are looking for documentation related to a plugin or module, please try one of the following links. If you were redirected to this page from an older version of the Ansible documentation, please also consider updating your bookmarks.
